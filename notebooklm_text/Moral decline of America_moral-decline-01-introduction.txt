There's an equation that describes how things fall apart.

Not metaphorically. Not poetically. Mathematically. The same structure that governs how a laser loses coherence, how a quantum system decoheres into noise, how an ecosystem collapses past its tipping point — that same equation applies to civilizations. To families. To you.

I didn't go looking for it. And somewhere around month eleven of that work, the data forced my hand.

The Master Equation

\[ \frac{dC}{dt} = O \cdot G(1 - C) - S \cdot C \]

The rate of change of coherence over time equals the observer's receptivity times the grace input times the room left to grow, minus the entropy times the current coherence.

Variable Definitions

C

Coherence — the structural integrity of a system, whether that system is a marriage, a church, or a republic.

G

Negentropic Input — the external force that pushes a system toward order. In physics, it's gravity. In theology, it's grace.

O

Observer Receptivity — the observer's capacity to receive the negentropic input.

S

Entropy Load — the forces of decay, disorder, dissolution.

When G is strong and S is manageable, systems cohere. They hold together. They produce order from chaos.

When S overwhelms G — or when O drops to zero and nobody is receiving the signal anymore — coherence collapses. And it collapses fast.

Stay with me here. Because I'm about to do something that will make some people uncomfortable.

I ran the equation on America.

The Five-Year Window

Between 1968 and 1973 — five years — the following events occurred simultaneously across six independent domains:

Family Structure

California legalized no-fault divorce (1969). Griswold and Eisenstadt decoupled sex from marriage through contraceptive access (1965, 1972). Roe v. Wade legalized abortion nationwide (1973). The divorce rate began its vertical climb toward a peak of 5.2 per thousand.

Institutional Trust

The Vietnam War peaked and exposed. The Pentagon Papers dropped (1971). Kent State happened (1970). Watergate broke (1972). Trust in government fell from 62% to 36% in four years.

Moral Standards

The Hays Code collapsed and was replaced by the MPAA rating system (1968). The Miller Test codified moral relativism into obscenity law (1973). Deep Throat entered mainstream theaters (1972). Playboy circulation hit 7 million, up 536% from 1965.

Religious Authority

Christian identification began its long slide from 87% (1973) toward 45% (2023). Church attendance dropped below 40%. The APA removed homosexuality from the DSM (December 15, 1973).

Language and Culture

"All in the Family" premiered with taboo language on broadcast TV (1971). The Controlled Substances Act formalized the drug war (1970). Profanity began its measurable migration from taboo to normal.

Economic Structure

Nixon took the dollar off the gold standard (1971). The relationship between currency and physical reality severed. Planned obsolescence became the dominant manufacturing philosophy.

Six domains. Five years. Simultaneous collapse. That is not a coincidence. That is a phase transition.

What the Equation Predicts

Run the coherence equation on this data and something precise falls out.

Before 1968, the negentropic input G was high. Churches were full. Families were intact. Institutions were trusted. The cultural signal — the shared moral vocabulary — was strong. The observer function O was high. People were receiving the signal. They were oriented toward it.

The entropy S existed — it always does — but it was contained. Managed. Absorbed by the system's coherence reserves.

Then, in a window narrower than any purely sociological model predicts, G dropped and S spiked simultaneously. The churches emptied. The families fractured. The institutions lied and got caught. The cultural signal degraded into noise. And the observer function — the capacity of the population to even receive a coherent moral signal — began its long decline toward zero.

The equation says: when O · G drops below the entropy threshold, coherence doesn't decline linearly. It collapses. The system undergoes a phase transition from ordered to disordered. And once through the transition, the new disordered state becomes self-reinforcing. Entropy feeds on the wreckage of coherence.

That's exactly what the data shows.
