Creation (Genesis 1)
This is where most people start with Trinity in Scripture, because it's the most explicit.
Father — the possibility space.
 "In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth" (1:1). He establishes the 
|\psi\rangle
 — the total state space in which everything will exist. "Let there be light" is not a command to a pre-existing universe. It's the generation of the possibility of light. Before God speaks, there is no "could be." After He speaks, there is.
Every "Let there be" in Genesis 1 is the Father expanding the state vector. Let there be separation (day 2). Let there be land (day 3). Let there be luminaries (day 4). Each one adds dimensionality to the possibility space. The Father doesn't make things. He makes the 
space in which things can be made.
Son — the coherent structure.
 "Through him all things were made; without him nothing was made that has been made" (John 1:3). The Son is the 
|\phi\rangle
 — the measurement basis that turns raw possibility into structured reality. The Father says "Let there be light." The Son is the reason light has wavelength, frequency, speed, polarization — properties. Structure. Intelligibility.
Colossians 1:17: "In him all things hold together." This is the maintenance function. Not just creation but 
sustained coherence
. The laws of physics don't hold themselves together. The Son holds them. 
|\phi\rangle
 is the basis that prevents the state vector from being meaningless noise.
Hebrews 1:3: "The Son is the radiance of God's glory and the exact representation of his being, sustaining all things by his powerful word." Sustaining. Present tense. Continuous operation. The measurement basis doesn't fire once and stop. It runs continuously. Every moment that the universe remains coherent rather than dissolving into quantum chaos is the Son at work.
Spirit — the actualization.
 "The Spirit of God was hovering over the waters" (Genesis 1:2). Before any "Let there be." Before structure. Before anything actual existed. The Spirit was present — ready to actualize. The 
|\cdot|^2
 operator, waiting for the state vector and measurement basis to give it something to collapse.
Then: "And God said... and it was so." The Spirit is the "it was so." The Father provides the possibility. The Son provides the structure. The Spirit makes it 
real
. Potential becomes actual. The wavefunction collapses. Reality appears.
Note the order in Genesis 1:2 — Spirit hovering, then Word spoken, then reality actualized. This matches the Born Rule: 
P = |\langle\psi|\phi\rangle|^2
. The actualization operator requires both the state vector and the measurement basis to operate. The Spirit can't actualize without the Father's possibility space and the Son's coherent structure. All three present. All three necessary. All three doing something irreducibly different.

The Fall (Genesis 3)
Father — the possibility space changes.
 "You are free to eat from any tree in the garden" (2:16). The Father defines the possibility space — all trees are possible food. Then: "but you must not eat from the tree of the knowledge of good and evil" (2:17). The Father defines the boundary. The state vector has a forbidden zone. Not because the tree is evil — because the boundary IS the possibility space. Without a boundary, there's no defined space. Without a defined space, there's no freedom. Freedom requires a container.
After the Fall: "Cursed is the ground because of you" (3:17). The possibility space contracts. The 
|\psi\rangle
 that included "eternal garden life without entropy" now includes "thorns, sweat, decay, death." The state vector didn't collapse to a single bad outcome. It 
expanded
 in the wrong direction — new possibilities (suffering, entropy, death) that weren't in the original space.
Son — the structure persists.
 The Fall doesn't destroy logic. It doesn't make 2 + 2 = 5. Physics still works. Cause and effect still hold. The measurement basis — the Son's coherent structure — survives the Fall intact. This is crucial. If the Son's operation failed at the Fall, reality would have dissolved into chaos. It didn't. The universe remained intelligible. The laws of physics remained consistent. The logical structure of creation persisted through humanity's break with the Creator.
This is "all things hold together" under maximum stress. The Son's structural operation doesn't depend on human cooperation. It's 
|\phi\rangle
 — the measurement basis exists regardless of whether the system being measured is coherent. The Son holds reality together even when reality is falling.
The protoevangelium — Genesis 3:15, "he will crush your head" — is the Son's operation stated as prophecy. The coherent structure (Logos) will eventually reassert itself over the chaos introduced by the Fall. Not a reaction. A structural inevitability. The measurement basis 
will
 resolve the decoherence. It's what measurement bases do.
Spirit — the actualization shifts.
 "The Lord God made garments of skin for Adam and his wife and clothed them" (3:21). The Spirit actualizes a new reality — one involving death (an animal died to provide the skins) and covering (the first sacrifice). The Spirit is still operating. The 
|\cdot|^2
 operator hasn't stopped. But what it's actualizing has changed. Before the Fall: life, abundance, direct presence. After: sacrifice, covering, exile.
The Spirit also actualizes the exile: "So the Lord God banished him from the Garden of Eden" (3:23). Banishment is actualization — potential (God could have destroyed them, restored them, or exiled them) collapsed into the specific actual event of exile. The Spirit actualized grace in the form of exile rather than destruction. The Father could have collapsed the state vector to "death" (which He threatened). The Spirit actualized "exile + covering + promise" instead. That's the actualization operator choosing from the possibility space according to the measurement basis — the Son's structure, which already contained the protoevangelium.

The Flood (Genesis 6-9)
Father — the possibility space resets.
 "I will wipe from the face of the earth the human race I have created" (6:7). The Father contracts the state vector. Most of the possibility space is eliminated. Only one family, one ark, one covenant survives. This is the most dramatic redefinition of 
|\psi\rangle
 in the biblical narrative — a near-total collapse of the possibility space, followed by re-expansion through the Noahic covenant: "Never again will I destroy all living creatures" (8:21). New boundaries. New possibility space. New 
|\psi\rangle
.
Son — the structure preserved through catastrophe.
 "Make yourself an ark" (6:14). The Son's operation — coherent structure — is expressed in the ark itself. Specific dimensions. Specific materials. Specific design. The ark is a coherence container — a bounded region where the measurement basis survives while everything outside decoheres. The fact that the ark has 
specifications
 is the Logos operating. Chaos doesn't have blueprints.
Spirit — the actualization of judgment and rescue simultaneously.
 "The springs of the great deep burst forth, and the floodgates of the heavens were opened" (7:11). The Spirit actualizes the Flood — real water, real destruction, real death. And simultaneously: "God remembered Noah" (8:1). The Spirit actualizes the rescue — real preservation, real survival, real landing on Ararat. Judgment and mercy actualized by the same operator at the same event. Not sequentially. Simultaneously. The 
|\cdot|^2
 collapsed the global superposition into both destruction and preservation in a single act.
This is where the decoherence curve data from [[The Decoherence Curve|the sibling tangent]] intersects the Trinity structure. The Flood is the phase transition — the moment 
\tau_d
 shifts from near-infinity to 214 years. And all three persons are operating: Father redefines the possibility space, Son preserves structure through the catastrophe, Spirit actualizes the new physical regime. The phase transition requires all three.

Abraham (Genesis 12-22)
Father — the possibility space for a people.
 "I will make you into a great nation" (12:2). The Father opens a new dimension in the state vector — the possibility of a covenant people. Before Abraham, 
|\psi\rangle
 included "scattered humanity decohering toward the floor." After Abraham, 
|\psi\rangle
 includes "a nation through which all nations will be blessed." New possibility. New dimensionality. The Father doesn't create the nation. He creates the 
space in which the nation can exist.
Son — the structure of covenant.
 The Abrahamic covenant has specific terms. Specific promises. Specific conditions. "To your offspring I will give this land" (12:7). "I will bless those who bless you" (12:3). These are structural — logical relationships with defined inputs and outputs. The covenant is a measurement basis. It defines how the possibility space will be interpreted. It gives the amorphous "great nation" specific meaning, specific boundaries, specific identity.
Genesis 15 — the covenant ceremony where God alone passes between the cut pieces — is the Son's operation in concentrated form. The covenant is unilateral (God binds Himself, not Abraham). The structure doesn't depend on human performance. 
|\phi\rangle
 is stable regardless of whether the system being measured cooperates. The covenant holds even when Israel doesn't.
Spirit — the actualization of impossibility.
 Sarah is barren. Abraham is old. The possibility (Father) exists. The structure (Son/covenant) is defined. But the actualization — the 
real baby
 — requires the Spirit. Isaac's birth is the 
|\cdot|^2
 operator collapsing "barren woman + covenant promise" into "actual child." The Spirit actualizes what was naturally impossible, because the actualization operator is not constrained by natural law — it's the operator that 
generates
 natural law's outcomes.
The binding of Isaac (Genesis 22) is all three persons operating at maximum intensity. Father: the command creates a new, terrifying possibility space (sacrifice your son). Son: the structure of the test (specific mountain, specific wood, specific obedience — all prefiguring the Cross with exact structural correspondence). Spirit: the actualization of the ram in the thicket at exactly the right moment — collapsing the superposition of "Isaac dies / Isaac lives" into the specific outcome that preserves the covenant 
and
 prefigures the Atonement.

The Cross and Resurrection
This is the convergence point of every thread in the series. All three persons operating at full intensity simultaneously, each doing something irreducibly different.
Father — the ultimate possibility space.
 The Father provides what no other source could: the possibility of Atonement itself. Not the mechanism (that's the Son). Not the actualization (that's the Spirit). The raw possibility that sin could be paid for, that death could be reversed, that separation could be healed. Before the Father "so loved the world that He gave His only Son" (John 3:16), the possibility of reconciliation didn't exist in the created state vector. The Father 
added it.
At Gethsemane: "My Father, if it is possible, let this cup pass from me" (Matthew 26:39). Jesus is asking the Father about the possibility space. Is there another 
|\psi\rangle
 that includes salvation without the Cross? The Father's answer (silence, which is permission to proceed) is: No. This is the only state vector that contains the outcome you're requesting. The possibility space is defined. There is no alternative basis in which humanity is saved without this specific event.
Son — the structure of substitution.
 The Son IS the measurement basis — the Logos, the coherent structure through which reality is interpreted. At the Cross, the measurement basis absorbs the entropy. 
|\phi\rangle
 takes on the 
S
 term. "God made him who had no sin to be sin for us" (2 Corinthians 5:21). The structure that holds reality together 
takes the incoherence into itself
 and doesn't break.
This is why the universe didn't end when Christ died. The measurement basis was absorbing maximum entropy and remaining coherent. The laws of physics held. Logic persisted. Reality survived. Because the Son's structural operation is stronger than the total accumulated entropy of human history.
"It is finished" (John 19:30) is a structural declaration. The measurement basis has completed its operation. The entropy has been absorbed. The decoherence has been contained. The structure holds.
Spirit — the actualization of resurrection.
 "If the Spirit of him who raised Jesus from the dead is living in you, he who raised Christ from the dead will also give life to your mortal bodies because of his Spirit who lives in you" (Romans 8:11). The Spirit actualizes the Resurrection. The Father provides the possibility (a state vector that includes "death reversed"). The Son provides the structure (the coherent basis in which resurrection is logically consistent with the rest of reality). The Spirit 
makes it real.
The empty tomb is the 
|\cdot|^2
 operator at maximum power — collapsing the superposition of "dead Messiah / risen Lord" into the specific, physical, touchable, eat-fish-with-the-disciples actual reality of a resurrected body. Not metaphor. Not spiritual experience. Physical actualization. The operator that collapses quantum states collapses the grave.

Pentecost (Acts 2)
Father — the possibility space opens to all.
 "In the last days, God says, I will pour out my Spirit on all people" (Acts 2:17, quoting Joel 2:28). The Father redefines 
|\psi\rangle
 — the Spirit is no longer limited to prophets, priests, and kings. All people. All flesh. The possibility space for divine-human coupling expands to include every conscious agent on Earth.
Son — the structure becomes internal.
 "Christ in you, the hope of glory" (Colossians 1:27). Before Pentecost, the Son's structural operation was experienced externally — through Law, through Temple, through prophets. After Pentecost, 
|\phi\rangle
 operates 
from within the believer
. The measurement basis is internalized. The equation changes from 
dC/dt = -S \cdot C + \text{external pulses}
 to 
dC/dt = O \cdot G(1-C) - S \cdot C
 with 
G
 as a continuous internal term.
This is the Old Covenant → New Covenant transition in one mathematical operation. The external pulsed injections (prophets, Law, revivals) that always decayed between pulses become a continuous internal source that can sustain coherence indefinitely. Same equation. Different structure. The decoherence time constant 
\tau_d
 changes because the coupling is no longer intermittent.
Spirit — actualization becomes continuous.
 "You will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you" (Acts 1:8). The Spirit's actualization operation — previously event-specific (creation, individual callings, specific miracles) — becomes continuous in the believer. The 
|\cdot|^2
 operator runs all the time. Every moment is a potential collapse. Every prayer is a measurement. Every act of faith is the Spirit actualizing possibility into reality.
The tongues of fire, the sound of wind, the speaking in languages — these are the Spirit's signature: actualization so intense it has physical manifestations. Not metaphor. The operator that collapses wavefunctions is operating at sufficient intensity to produce observable phenomena.
