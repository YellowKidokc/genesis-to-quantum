Each law is not an analogy but a claim: the physics and the theology share identical mathematical structure, testable by variable substitution (see [[MA-00_Master-Equation#[Isomorphism Falsification](https://www.oxfordreference.com/view/10.1093/acref/isomorphism+falsification) Criterion]]).

### Reading the Table

Each law connects a physics domain to a spiritual mirror through a shared χ variable. The symmetry pair column shows which Z₂ mirror duality (from [[MA-00_Master-Equation#Five-Fold Mirror Duality]]) governs that law's dynamics. Laws sharing a symmetry pair are structurally coupled — they constrain each other's predictions.

## Internal Structure of Each Law

Every law follows a six-part structure (established in the Wolfram Deep Laws rewrite):

1. **[Physical Law](https://www.oxfordreference.com/view/10.1093/acref/physical+law)** — The physics principle with its mathematical formalism
2. **Spiritual Mirror** — The theological parallel with scriptural grounding
3. **Meaning** — Why this pairing is structurally necessary, not metaphorical
4. **Master Equation Connection** — How this law's variable enters χ
5. **Trinity Anchor** — Which person of the Trinity this law most closely maps to
6. **Historical Echoes** — Where this pairing has appeared in intellectual history

Each law also contains an internal bifurcation ([Newton](https://www.oxfordreference.com/view/10.1093/acref/newton) pole ↔ [[Einstein]] ([Oxford Reference](https://www.oxfordreference.com/view/10.1093/acref/einstein)) pole) mapping to a sin ↔ grace polarity within the variable itself. See [[MA-00_Master-Equation#Internal Bifurcation Pattern]].
