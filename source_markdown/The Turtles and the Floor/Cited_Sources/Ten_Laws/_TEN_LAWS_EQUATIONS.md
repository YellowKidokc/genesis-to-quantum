# The Ten Laws & Their Equations

## Law 1: Law of Grace (G)

```
∂χ/∂G = K
δχ/δG = ∫K dΩ
```

*Grace couples to Knowledge*

**Physical Analog:** [[Einstein]] ([Oxford Reference](https://www.oxfordreference.com/view/10.1093/acref/einstein)) field equations with χ-term
```
G_μν + Λg_μν = (8πG_N/c⁴)[T_μν^(matter) + T_μν^(W) + T_μν^(χ)]
```

## Law 2: Law of Mass (M)

```
∂²χ/∂x² = -ρ_info = -M/V
∇²χ = ∫∇²(G·K) dΩ = -4πρ_info
```

*Mass is information density*

**Physical Analog:** Strong force exponential
```
V(r) = -g²/r · e^(-mr)
```

## Law 3: Law of Energy (E)

```
E = ∫T·dχ
```

*Energy is time-integrated coherence change*

```

```

*Natural decay toward decoherence*

**Physical Analog:** Second [Law of Thermodynamics](https://www.oxfordreference.com/view/10.1093/acref/law+of+thermodynamics)
