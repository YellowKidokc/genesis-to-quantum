## The Job Disruption Vector: Digital vs Physical AI

The current fracture is being accelerated by a specific technological force diagram:

**Digital AI → Democrat Identity:** LLMs disrupt digital jobs — journalists, doctors, lawyers, bureaucrats, creative professionals. These are the roles Blue America uses to define its social stature. Blue states react with "legal guild" rulings and restrictive AI laws.

**Physical AI → Republican Identity:** Robots and automated manufacturing disrupt the physical world — Red America's domain. The "China singularity" already flipped Republican-aligned domestic manufacturing in the 2010s.

Digital AI is currently hitting Democrat strongholds harder than Physical AI is hitting the heartland. Result: "tech-lash" from the Left mirrors the "China-lash" from the Right.
